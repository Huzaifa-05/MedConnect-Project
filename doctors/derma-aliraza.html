About Dr. Ali Raza

Dr. Ali Raza is a highly experienced and compassionate Dermatologist with 9 years of practice dedicated to skin health. He is known for his patient-centered approach and expertise in diagnosing and treating a wide range of dermatological conditions, including acne, eczema, psoriasis, and skin cancer. Dr. Raza believes in empowering his patients with knowledge and works closely with them to develop personalized treatment plans that promote long-term skin health.

He completed his MBBS from Allama Iqbal Medical College and his FCPS in Dermatology from College of Physicians and Surgeons Pakistan. He regularly attends national and international conferences to stay updated with the latest advancements in dermatology.
