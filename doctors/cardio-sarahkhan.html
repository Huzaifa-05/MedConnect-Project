About Dr. Sarah Khan

Dr. Sarah Khan is a highly experienced and compassionate Cardiologist with 12 years of practice dedicated to heart health. She is known for her patient-centered approach and expertise in diagnosing and treating a wide range of cardiovascular conditions, including coronary artery disease, heart failure, arrhythmias, and hypertension. Dr. Khan believes in empowering her patients with knowledge and works closely with them to develop personalized treatment plans that promote long-term well-being.

She completed her MBBS from Dow University of Health Sciences and her FCPS in Cardiology from Aga Khan University. She regularly attends national and international conferences to stay updated with the latest advancements in cardiology.
