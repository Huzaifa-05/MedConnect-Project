About Dr. Shaaf Ali

Dr. Shaaf Ali is a highly experienced and compassionate Cardiologist with 10 years of practice dedicated to heart health. He is known for his patient-centered approach and expertise in diagnosing and treating a wide range of cardiovascular conditions, including coronary artery disease, heart failure, arrhythmias, and hypertension. Dr. Raza believes in empowering his patients with knowledge and works closely with them to develop personalized treatment plans that promote long-term well-being.

He completed his MBBS from King Edward Medical University and his FCPS in Cardiology from College of Physicians and Surgeons Pakistan. He regularly attends national and international conferences to stay updated with the latest advancements in cardiology.
