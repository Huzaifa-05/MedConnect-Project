About Dr. Hassan Javed

Dr. Hassan Javed is a highly experienced and compassionate Dentist with 8 years of practice dedicated to dental health. He is known for his patient-centered approach and expertise in diagnosing and treating a wide range of dental conditions, including orthodontics, oral surgery, and preventive care. Dr. Javed believes in empowering his patients with knowledge and works closely with them to develop personalized treatment plans that promote long-term oral health.

He completed his BDS from University of Health Sciences and his FCPS in Orthodontics from College of Physicians and Surgeons Pakistan. He regularly attends national and international conferences to stay updated with the latest advancements in dentistry.
