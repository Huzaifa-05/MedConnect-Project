About Dr. Ayesha Malik

Dr. Ayesha Malik is a highly experienced and compassionate Pediatrician with 14 years of practice dedicated to children's health. She is known for her patient-centered approach and expertise in diagnosing and treating a wide range of pediatric conditions, including infectious diseases, growth disorders, asthma, and developmental issues. Dr. Malik believes in empowering parents with knowledge and works closely with families to develop personalized care plans that promote long-term health and well-being for children.

She completed her MBBS from Lahore Medical and Dental College and her FCPS in Pediatrics from College of Physicians and Surgeons Pakistan. She regularly attends national and international conferences to stay updated with the latest advancements in pediatric medicine.
