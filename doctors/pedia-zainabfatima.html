About Dr. Zainab Fatima

Dr. Zainab Fatima is a highly experienced and compassionate Pediatrician with 11 years of practice dedicated to children's health. She is known for her patient-centered approach and expertise in diagnosing and treating a wide range of pediatric conditions, including infectious diseases, nutrition disorders, allergies, and behavioral issues. Dr. Fatima believes in empowering parents with knowledge and works closely with families to develop personalized care plans that promote long-term health and well-being for children.

She completed her MBBS from Services Institute of Medical Sciences and her FCPS in Pediatrics from College of Physicians and Surgeons Pakistan. She regularly attends national and international conferences to stay updated with the latest advancements in pediatric medicine.
