About Dr. Hira Qureshi

Dr. Hira Qureshi is a highly experienced and compassionate Dentist with 7 years of practice dedicated to dental health. She is known for her patient-centered approach and expertise in diagnosing and treating a wide range of dental conditions, including prosthodontics, cosmetic dentistry, and preventive care. Dr. Qureshi believes in empowering her patients with knowledge and works closely with them to develop personalized treatment plans that promote long-term oral health.

She completed her BDS from Fatima Jinnah Dental College and her MDS in Prosthodontics from de'Montmorency College of Dentistry. She regularly attends national and international conferences to stay updated with the latest advancements in dentistry.
