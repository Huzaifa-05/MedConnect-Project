About Dr. Bilal Tariq

Dr. Bilal Tariq is a highly experienced and compassionate Neurologist with 13 years of practice dedicated to brain and nervous system health. He is known for his patient-centered approach and expertise in diagnosing and treating a wide range of neurological conditions, including epilepsy, stroke, Parkinson's disease, and migraines. Dr. Tariq believes in empowering his patients with knowledge and works closely with them to develop personalized treatment plans that promote long-term neurological health.

He completed his MBBS from Nishtar Medical College and his FRCP in Neurology from Royal College of Physicians, UK. He regularly attends national and international conferences to stay updated with the latest advancements in neurology.
