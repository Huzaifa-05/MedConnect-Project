About Dr. Nida Shah

Dr. Nida Shah is a highly experienced and compassionate Neurologist with 10 years of practice dedicated to brain and nervous system health. She is known for her patient-centered approach and expertise in diagnosing and treating a wide range of neurological conditions, including epilepsy, stroke, multiple sclerosis, and headaches. Dr. Shah believes in empowering her patients with knowledge and works closely with them to develop personalized treatment plans that promote long-term neurological health.

She completed her MBBS from Rawalpindi Medical College and her FCPS in Neurology from College of Physicians and Surgeons Pakistan. She regularly attends national and international conferences to stay updated with the latest advancements in neurology.
